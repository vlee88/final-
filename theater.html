Home theater

A luxury home theater is limited only by your imagination. You can have a superior design that surpasses the impact and realism of the best commercial cinemas. Outstanding features include special acoustics, large screens, drapery, lighting, carpet, plush seating and easy-to-use control panels. Our innovative home theaters are designed adapt to any space in your house. Whether you are building a new home, remodeling a spare room or completing an unfinished basement, we will create the perfect environment for enjoying a movie, watching a concert or viewing a sporting event. You will feel like you're in a commercial theater, but in the comfort of your own home.

Enjoy.

Our specially designed media centers feature superb audio and high-definition video. Every family member will receive the same quality experience from anywhere in the room. In addition to family room enjoyment, we can eliminate the clutter usually found in media rooms by concealing the electronic components in an attractive cabinet. Speakers can be flush mounted into the walls and ceilings. Flat screens can be framed with artwork that scrolls up and down with a remote control.
